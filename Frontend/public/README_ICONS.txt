Place your logo files here and name them exactly as referenced in index.html:

- logo.png            (recommended 512x512 PNG) - used as primary favicon
- logo32.png          (32x32 PNG)
- logo16.png          (16x16 PNG)
- apple-touch-icon.png (180x180 PNG for iOS)

After adding your images, rebuild/restart Vite (if needed) and clear cache on your iPhone (Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data) to see the updated icon.
